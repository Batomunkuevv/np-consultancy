Why the UAE?

Free
economic zones

The UAE has a lot of free zones, each with its own regulations for doing business. Each free zone offers entrepreneurs an opportunity to fully own foreign capital without involving local sponsors, benefit from the exemption from taxes and export/import duties, and enjoy access to opening businesses without a stack of government permits.

Tax
incentives

The Emirates’ tax system is one of the most loyal in the world. An individual who is a UAE resident is not subject to any taxes and can obtain documents to evidence his or her status as a local tax resident. For businesses, the corporate tax of 9% is only applicable to profits above AED 375 thousand ($102 thousand).

Strategic geographical
location

The UAE is located at the crossroads of trade routes between Europe, Asia and Africa. This makes the country a perfect place for engaging in trade and logistics internationally, as well as for entering the fast-growing markets in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) region.

Well-developed
infrastructure

The UAE boasts first-class infrastructure, including modern airports, seaports, highways, office and residential buildings, telecommunications and Internet connection facilities that meet the highest standards.

Security
and comfort

The UAE is known for its high standards of living and security. The country offers an enabling environment not only for doing business, but also for living, which is an important factor for business people considering a relocation.

FAQ

  • How long does it take to get a visa?

    The standard time for obtaining a visa is 2 weeks.

  • How long does it take to open a bank account or a business?

    Opening a bank account takes 3 weeks. Opening a company takes at least 3 days in the mainland and at least one week in a free zone.

  • Do you provide assistance with collecting the required documents?

    We will provide you with a full list of documents for opening a company and obtaining a visa. We can also help you with preparing documents for opening a corporate account with a number of banks.

  • What are the advantages of the Golden Visa?

    The UAE’s Golden Visa is a long-term residence visa for foreign investors, entrepreneurs, professionals, and their family members. It enables them and their families to live in the UAE for 5 or 10 years, whereupon it can be renewed. A Golden Visa holder is not required to stay in the UAE for a certain minimum period of time and enjoys easier access to banking services, mortgages and other finance options.
